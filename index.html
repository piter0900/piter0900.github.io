I'm who I am

I feel greatful for what I have--especially when I live in a place of freedom and peace.

I am a spark-- full of Energy and passion, and I light up the room with it.

I can cohere a team with my ability to communicate and my positivity.

I’m up for any challenges, because learning is what I’m supposed to do, what I’m good at, and becoming better is my greatest addiction that I cannot shake free.

I have high hopes for myself, and with that come high standards for my product.

I love experimenting, but I can always find the way back if something goes wrong.

I believe that I can do anything I set my mind to, and as long as I keep trying, and that no failures will override my effort because that’s how I learn.

I believe that at the age of 21, literally anything that happens will help me learn in one way or another if I stay humble and hungry.

I believe I can do great things- as long as I stay active and initiative

I live in the present—what happens now is the most important thing that will ever happen.

Yet I plan the future—--no direction, no destination arrived.
